Education

Diplomas and Certifications


East Central University of southern Oklahoma is my alma mater. I was able to achieve a degree in computer science, with a minor in Russian language studies. I would say overall I enjoyed my time at university, but there are things I wish I would've done or could've done. However, that doesn't mean that I can't! Continuing education is a big part of who I am. Whether that's my continued study of foregin languages, pursuit of various certifications or just my study of mathematics for pleasure learning is a deeply important part of who I am.


While at university, I also had the opportunity to be the president of the ECU Russian club for four years. It was an honor, and I learned about leadership through that role, and it helped me learn how to ask others for help and not be so afraid of making mistakes, as long as I learned. I was also a member of the national slavic honor society Dobro Slovo, and was the president of it as well for two years.


Outside of college, I have acheived a certificate in data analytics from Coursera through Google. This taught me about the data analysis process, from asking questions, to taking action with the data that you've aquired. A few youtube videos about data analytics got me into the idea of it, but this course really got me actively using it in my everday life. It helps me in little ways everyday, and it can help anyone if they pay attention. The most life altering thing it has done for me has to be the amazing amount of weight I've lost.
