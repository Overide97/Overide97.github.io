    • RPG Population Generator

      • I like Dungeons and Dragons, and while being the dungeon master and working, I noticed I didn't always have time to create worlds and entire populations. I decided to use Python to solve my problem. This code will generate five thousand different NPCs for my campaign. Since this takes place in Saltmarsh on The Sword Coast in The Forgotten Realms, I decided to also make sure that the weights accurately refelct that. Halflings are more likely to be farmers, and the most common jobs that you will find in town will involve the local fishing trade. It also factors in the fictional ethnicities of the setting as well, for every race, along with their age and gender. If they end up an adventurer, it will take that into account and give them a class such as fighter or wizard.
